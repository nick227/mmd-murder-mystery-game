## Playability burn-down

Single source of truth for playtest blockers. Keep this focused on **playability**, not architecture.

### ❌ act clarity
- [ ] Players understand when an act changes (what changed + what to do next)
- [ ] “New this act” is correctly scoped and ordered

### ❌ objective confusion
- [ ] Objectives are clearly “instructions” vs “flavor”
- [ ] Completing an objective matches expected gameplay intent

### ❌ card overload
- [ ] Clues/instructions don’t overwhelm players (grouping/sorting)
- [ ] Long cards remain readable on phone

### ❌ host flow
- [ ] Host always knows what to read/do now (speech + next act)
- [ ] Host can confirm players joined

### ❌ puzzle visibility
- [ ] Puzzle cards appear at the right time
- [ ] Unlock cards appear when puzzle is solved (MVP: after submitObjective)

### ❌ join UX
- [ ] Join screen is obvious and fast
- [ ] Host can see who joined
